About Us

PAN de Special serves handcrafted special flavors with special bread and spreads.

Our objective for Pan de Special is to be available to every client in the Philippines on a daily basis. Pan de Special separates itself from other bakeries by being significantly less expensive and more affordable for customers. Even if our goods are inexpensive, we guarantee that they are of high quality, and we will adapt to the most popular tastes, product types, and current trends so that customers may enjoy Pan de Special at all times and seasons.

How did Pan de Special come into being? It was established by Group 13 on April 14, 2024. We came up with the notion of opening a bakery. So that any customers, including loyal customers, can come.
